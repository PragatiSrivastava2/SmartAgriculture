About Us

We are building a website that focuses on solving the problems faced by farmers, such as:

  • Unreachability to government schemes
  • Lack of advanced knowledge
  • Less earning due to the involvement of middlemen
  • Reduction in selling price due to not having proper storage to store their grains

Our goal is to provide solutions to these problems through our platform.
